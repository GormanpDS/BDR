# Dynamo BDR: Role Essence & High-Impact Actions

> A focused cheat sheet for top-performing BDRs in hedge fund sales at Dynamo. Omit the basics; double-down on what drives pipeline and results.

---

## 🏹 Core Mission
- Own the **top of the funnel**: fill the sales pipeline with qualified, well-researched opportunities for Dynamo’s Hedge Fund segment.
- Contribute *tangible value*—measured by quality SQLs that convert, not just activity.

## 🔑 Responsibilities (What Outperforms)
- **Prospecting & Multi-Channel Outreach**
  - Craft account lists, prioritize hottest segments.
  - Execute smart, multi-touch cadences across email, phone, LinkedIn, events.
- **Qualification & Discovery**
  - Understand fund needs, prioritize pain (reporting, data, workflow, investor relations, etc.).
  - Transition from interest → MQL → SQL. Always be upgrading the quality of your funnel.
- **Advance & Handoff**
  - Pass only "ready" leads.
  - Deliver clean, detailed handoff context to SRs (history, triggers, urgency, politics).
- **Territory Ownership**
  - Plan and strategize territory; don’t “spray and pray.”
  - Use data and QBRs to assess gaps and overweights. Rebalance frequently.
- **Relentless Follow-Up & Nurture**
  - Track every touch. Value-based persistence (education, resources, timely insights).
  - Segment and cycle—never drop hot/warm leads.

## 📊 Performance Levers & Metrics
| KPI / Skill                    | High-Impact Target                  |
|-------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| SQLs / Pipeline Adds           | The main event (core to comp)       |
| Meetings Set                   | Track → Tune → Increase             |
| Call/Email/Touch Volumes       | Only meaningful if conversion rises  |
| Lead-to-SQL & Call-to-Meeting  | Benchmark, A/B test, improve        |
| Territory Coverage & Gaps      | Run territory like a mini-business  |

- **Crush Quota**: Don’t just aim for baseline, aim to lead the team and set process improvements in motion.

## ⚡ Success Enablers (What Moves the Needle)
- **Personalization at Scale:** Always reference real fund context, news, or pain.
- **Cadence & Tracking Systems:** Leverage CRM, dashboards, follow-up rigorously.
- **Collaboration:** Get/Share feedback with SRs and peers. Learn what closed (and what didn’t).
- **Continuous Iteration:** Test subject lines, case study usage, call scripts, cadence timing. Iterate what works (share wins, retire what doesn’t).
- **Resourcefulness:** Tap Dynamo playbooks, case studies, peer calls, and industry news. Use thought leadership to stay top-of-mind.
- **Proactive Mindset:** Run your funnel like it’s your own book of business. If something’s not working, change it and document for the team.

## 🔁 Adaptable Weekly Blueprint (abbreviated)
- Block time for proactive calls/emails (AM best)
- All follow-ups tracked (no lead forgotten)
- At least 1-2 experiments in process or messaging running each week
- Prep territory review for Friday; recalibrate for wins next week

## 🔮 How This Pays Off
- Higher pipeline conversion
- More strategic (and larger) deals
- Recognition/advancement for leadership in process improvement
- More wins in less time
