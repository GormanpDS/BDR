## Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Personalize with fund strategy/recent news
- Lead with specific time savings or efficiency gains
- Keep to 3-4 sentences max
- Include clear CTA (meeting/call request)
- Reference similar fund success (if applicable)

❌ **Avoid:**
- Generic "hedge fund" language
- Lengthy feature lists
- Asking if they're "open to exploring"
- Too much urgency or pressure
